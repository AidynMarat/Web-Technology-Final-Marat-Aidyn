You can enjoy this melody while you study the history of our restaurant!

In a famous Japanese estate lived a samurai devoted to his family and his valor. The samurai was a respected guardian of the estate. One day, robbers invaded the estate, seeking to steal and destroy everything in their path. A samurai, ready to protect his family at any cost, stood in their way. He fought with courage and determination to prevent the attack, and eventually sacrificing his life saved Takumi and his family from death. As a sign of respect for his valor, the last descendant of the family opened a restaurant "Samurai Sake Restaurant" at the same place. This place has become not only a symbol of heroism and devotion, but also a place where guests can enjoy excellent cuisine and the delightful heritage of Japanese culture, which was passed on by the samurai and which Takumi continued to keep

This is the last descendant and owner of our restaurant - Haruki Sora. It was his initiative to create a restaurant on the site of the old estate and its name in honor of that beautiful courage of the samurai
